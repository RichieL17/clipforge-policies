ClipForge Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ClipForge handles your information. ClipForge is a personal desktop application for creating and uploading short-form videos to social media platforms including TikTok.

1. Information ClipForge Collects

ClipForge does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to any remote server. All data remains on your local device. Specifically, ClipForge stores:

  • TikTok OAuth access and refresh tokens (saved locally in your app's auth/ folder)
  • Your app configuration and preferences (saved locally)

2. TikTok API Usage

ClipForge uses the TikTok Content Posting API and Login Kit to authenticate your TikTok account and upload videos on your behalf. ClipForge only requests the following TikTok permissions:

  • user.info.basic — to verify account identity
  • video.publish — to upload videos to your TikTok account

ClipForge does not read, store, or share your TikTok profile data, followers, messages, or any other account information beyond what is needed to complete a video upload.

3. Data Sharing

ClipForge does not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party. No analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry is collected by ClipForge.

4. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored locally and can be deleted at any time by removing the auth/ folder from your ClipForge installation. Revoking access via your TikTok account settings will also invalidate any stored tokens.
